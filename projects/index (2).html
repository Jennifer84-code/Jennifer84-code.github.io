Senior Living Decision Guide

Making decisions about senior living can feel overwhelming. This guide will help you explore your options through a few simple questions, providing personalized recommendations based on your unique situation and needs.

Question 1 of 5

What level of daily assistance is needed?

Think about everyday activities like bathing, dressing, meal preparation, and medication management.

Minimal to no assistance - Independent living with occasional help
Some assistance - Help with a few daily tasks, but mostly independent
Regular assistance - Daily help with multiple activities
Full-time care - Comprehensive support with most daily activities
Question 2 of 5

How important is social engagement and activities?

Consider preferences for community events, group activities, and opportunities to stay socially connected.

Very important - Prefers a vibrant community with many activities
Moderately important - Enjoys some activities but values quiet time too
Less important - Prefers a quieter, more private environment
Question 3 of 5

Are there specific health or medical needs to consider?

This helps identify communities with appropriate medical support and specialized care services.

Memory care needs - Alzheimer's or dementia support
Chronic health conditions - Ongoing medical management required
Mobility challenges - Wheelchair accessible environment needed
Generally healthy - No significant medical concerns
Question 4 of 5

What's your timeline for making this transition?

Understanding urgency helps prioritize immediate availability versus exploring all options.

Immediate - Within 1-3 months
Soon - Within 3-6 months
Planning ahead - 6+ months or just exploring options
Question 5 of 5

What's most important in choosing a community?

This helps us understand your priorities and values in making this decision.

Quality of care - Staff training, medical support, care standards
Community and culture - Feeling at home, making connections
Location - Close to family, familiar area, accessibility
Value and affordability - Transparent pricing, financial options
